## Recommended Approach for Research

### 1. Start with Indigenous Voices
- Seek out Cree and Algonquian scholars
- Read Indigenous-authored works first
- Attend to community perspectives and concerns

### 2. Understand Cultural Context
- Learn about Algonquian cultures and history
- Understand survival conditions and ethics
- Recognize sacred nature of teachings

### 3. Recognize Colonial Impact
- Understand how colonialism distorted understanding
- Recognize ongoing harm from appropriation
- Question non-Indigenous interpretations

### 4. Respect Limitations
- Acknowledge what you cannot fully understand from outside the culture
- Do not claim expertise
- Defer to Indigenous knowledge keepers
